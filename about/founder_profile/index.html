Founder’s Profile

Mr. Tng Yew Beng, Ming — Founding Teacher (汤友明老师)

Teaching Philosophy & Vision

Every child and youth deserves an all-round academic, cultural, and foundational education through senior adolescence — preparing them for adulthood as quality people, not merely workers. This spirit is the raison d’être of Ruhan College: a developmental space to relish the joys of learning and a lasting beacon for the truths in Education.

Mr. Tng Yew Beng, Ming

Founding Teacher, Ruhan College

Biology & BiochemistryEducational NeuroscienceCognitive PsychologyBilingual Education

Journey & Experience

Undergraduate years in Australia rekindled his love for the Sciences, especially Biology and Biochemistry. Experiencing inspired teaching and learning, he undertook neuro-toxicology research that impressed his supervisors and earned another High Distinction.

Professionally, he served in Food Microbiology and Environmental Public Health while completing international certifications in Fitness and Aqua Instruction. Later, he read a Master of Arts in Brisbane focused on Broadcast Journalism and English Language & Literature, producing and narrating Mandarin news and current-affairs stories at MediaCorp News and Channel NewsAsia.

Building on a tertiary programme in Chinese/English Linguistics and Translation, he gained accreditation as a professional translator and trained industry professionals in Sustainability and Environmental Management. Returning to academia for a Master of Teaching, he developed interests in Educational Neuroscience and Cognitive Psychology, earning a Distinction for his thesis on qualitative methods in student engagement and outcomes.

In Far-North Queensland, he taught across K-12 in a state school, progressing to Full Registration with the Queensland College of Teachers. Across 18 years, his work has spanned professional development, classroom teaching, and educational research in both Australia and Singapore — culminating in his role as Founding Teacher at Ruhan College.

Beyond the classroom, he is a Hwa Chong alumnus and trained bass-baritone who continues classical singing under opera mentors in Singapore and Australia.

Highlights

  • Undergraduate studies in Sydney & Melbourne reignited a passion for Biology and Biochemistry; research project in Neuro-toxicology earned High Distinction.
  • Professional experience in Food Microbiology and Environmental Public Health; concurrently obtained international Fitness & Aqua Instructor certifications.
  • Broadcast Journalist with MediaCorp News and Channel NewsAsia producing/narrating Chinese-language news and current affairs.
  • Accredited Chinese/English Professional Translator; conducted Professional Development workshops on Sustainability and Environmental Management.
  • Graduate research and practicum toward Master of Teaching; academic interests in Educational Neuroscience and Cognitive Psychology.
  • Taught Mandarin to working adults at the Australian School of Contemporary Chinese; earned overall Distinction for Master’s thesis on qualitative research methods.
  • K-12 teaching in Far-North Queensland; progressed from Beginning Teacher to Full Registration with the Queensland College of Teachers.
  • 18 years across professional development, classroom teaching, and educational research in Australia and Singapore; now Founding Teacher at Ruhan College.
